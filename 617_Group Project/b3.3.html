Body Meridian

Jin Qiao Mai

Identified Active Components

Wild buckwheat rhizome clears heat, eliminates toxic substance, aids pus drainage, relieves swelling, dispels wind-dampness.Wild buckwheat rhizome is used in conditions like throat soreness, bronchitis, pus-forming infections in the chest region, dysentery, joint problems, menstrual pain or excessive vaginal discharges in women. It is also used tropically in skin sores and injuries.


Pharmacological Actions

Wild buckwheat rhizome promotes the healing of lung abscess and dissolves sputum to facilitate expectoration. It can be used alone, or combined with herbs like heartleaf houttuymia herb, honeysuckle flower and reed stem. Wild buckwheat rhizome clears heat, and promotes lung functioning, which help relieve cough and soothe throat. It usually works with heartleaf houttuymia herb and blackberrykiky rhizome for this. In TCM, a sluggish movement in the limbs or joints are usually due to wind-dampness accumulation, wild buckwheat rhizome can work with mulberry twig, star jasmine vine and atractylodes rhizome for treatment. Clinically, modern TCM also uses wild buckwheat rhizome to treat pharyngitis, tonsillitis, pneumonia, chronic bronchitis, pleurisy, and respiratory cancers.
