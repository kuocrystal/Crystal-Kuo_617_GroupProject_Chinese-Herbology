Body Meridian

Shan Yao

Identified Active Components

Chinese yam is neutral in nature and sweet in flavor. Active components in Chinese yam include mucopolysaccharides, diosgenin, allantoin, batatasin I, saponins, dopamine, essential amino acids, flavonoids, and amylase.


Pharmacological Actions

Chinese yam is mainly used in spleen and stomach weakness, poor appetite, diarrhea and excessive vaginal discharges. Other conditions such as chronic coughing due to lung weakness; or kidney weakness that leads to nocturnal emission, urinary frequency, excessive thirst or hunger are also indicated. Since the curative effects of Chinese yaw is mild, it usually acts as an associate ingredient, cannot exerts the major and leading effects in a formula. However, this makes it a popular dietary ingredient or tonic among the general public.
